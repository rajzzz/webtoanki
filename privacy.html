Privacy Policy – Web To Anki

Last updated: July 25, 2025

Web To Anki is a browser extension designed to help learners convert website content into flashcards for spaced repetition. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data.

What We Collect

  • Extension settings saved locally via chrome.storage.local
  • API keys or tokens you manually input (stored locally only)
  • Text you highlight or interact with (only when you trigger flashcard creation)
  • No background tracking or automatic data collection

How We Use It

  • Text and images you select are only sent to our backend or LLM service when you ask to create a flashcard
  • Your API key is used solely for model access (e.g., Gemini, OpenAI) and is never shared
  • No data is shared with third parties or advertisers

Third-Party Services

For Pro features, the extension may communicate with:

  • Gemini / OpenAI APIs (using your own API key)
  • Our hosted backend for Pro-only logic

Your Rights & Control

  • You may delete all extension data at any time via your browser’s extension settings
  • We store no persistent user data on our servers unless explicitly required (e.g., Pro unlock tokens)
